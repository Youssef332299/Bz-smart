# Bz smart

Flutter project.
Our application is designed to deliver a smooth and modern experience by combining powerful features with an intuitive interface.

The platform connects users with trusted service providers, making it easy to discover services, communicate, and complete transactions in just a few steps.

Key Features:

• Service Providers System – Users can browse and connect with verified providers offering a variety of services.
• Google Maps Integration – Easily find nearby providers and navigate to their locations using real-time Google Maps.
• Secure Online Payments – Pay safely through integrated electronic payment methods for a fast and reliable checkout experience.
• In-App Advertising Support – The app supports advertisements to help keep the platform accessible while promoting relevant services.
• Smooth Animations – Modern animations enhance the user interface and create a dynamic, enjoyable experience.
• User-Friendly Interface – Clean design and easy navigation ensure a seamless experience for all users.

Our goal is to provide a reliable, fast, and engaging platform that connects people with the services they need anytime and anywhere.
